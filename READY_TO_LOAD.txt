Option C: Manual Steps
  1. Open Firefox -> about:debugging#/runtime/this-firefox
  2. Click "Load Temporary Add-on"
  3. Select: builds\stream-sniper-firefox-v2.0.zip
  4. Copy the Extension ID from debugging page
  5. Edit: %APPDATA%\StreamSniper\com.streamsniper.ytdlp.json
  6. Replace {EXTENSION_ID_PLACEHOLDER} with your ID
  7. Restart Firefox and reload extension
